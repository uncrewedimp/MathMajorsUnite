About Page

We Process Your Data

We take the data you feed us and chop it into portions for model creation, then standardize and normalize it. Doing this allows us to prevent skewing from different measuring types and/or scales.

We Make You a Classification Model

We use a variety of machine learning techniques with your processed data to generate as accurate a model as possible (without overfitting).

We Run the Model on New Data

You want to predict how your customers or products will act based on the attributes you had. We take your data, process it, and then feed you back the predictions.
